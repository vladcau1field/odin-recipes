ginger green tea

description

ginger green tea is a refreshing beverage combining green tea’s and ginger’s health benefits. it is prepared by infusing green tea leaves and fresh ginger in hot water. the tea is known for its potential health benefits, including improved digestion, enhanced immune system, better cardiovascular health, and weight loss.

ingredients

  • ginger – to make this tea, use organic fresh ginger root. if fresh ginger is not available, you can use ground ginger. read about the benefits of ginger here.
  • green tea – i used green tea bags, but you can also use loose tea leaves. read about the benefits of green tea here.
  • lime juice – freshly squeezed lime juice gives a nice tang to the tea.
  • sweetener – use any sweetener of your choice.

steps

  1. put 2 and ½ cups of water in a saucepan and add 1 tablespoon grated ginger. bring to a boil over medium-high heat. once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 5 minutes. this will allow the ginger to infuse the water.
  2. after 5 minutes, turn off the heat and add 1 green tea bag (or 1 tablespoon loose tea leaves). cover the saucepan and let it steep for 1 minute.
  3. strain the tea into serving cups or a teapot, removing the ginger pieces and the tea leaves.add your preferred sweetener to taste and 1 teaspoon freshly squeezed lime juice, and stir well.

serve hot.
